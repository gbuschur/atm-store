Why it exists

AI can't work with a PDF. It can work with this.

Aviation transitions run on unstructured data — emails, PDFs, handwritten notes, 30-year-old paper records. AI has no clean entry point. ATM creates the structured layer it needs: consistent, organized, and platform agnostic. Use it with any AI tool you want, or none at all. The structure is the constant.

01

Structured. Not a black box.

ATM runs in Smartsheet — the spreadsheet your team already understands. No new software to learn. No black box to trust. Just structured, visible data.

02

AI-ready from day one.

Every finding, phase, record, and open item lives in consistent structured fields. When you're ready to plug in AI — it has somewhere clean to work and somewhere clean to land.

03

Platform agnostic.

ATM doesn't bet on one AI platform winning. The connection layer sits on top of the structure. Bring your own AI, or don't. The workspace works either way.

04

The lessor sets the standard.

When you own the workspace, you control the playing field. Every party — buyer, inspector, MRO — works inside your structure. Not their spreadsheet. Yours.
