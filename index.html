Process

How a transaction works

From the moment a deal opens to final re-induction, ATM tracks every finding, document, and sign-off.

01

Transaction workspace provisioned

Aerofield opens a dedicated 17-sheet workspace for your aircraft. Identity, engine records, landing gear, and AD status pre-loaded from available source documents.

02

Physical inspection & records review

Our inspector conducts on-site inspection and records review. Findings entered in real time — visible to all authorized parties immediately.

03

Open items tracked to resolution

Every finding logged in the Open Items Ledger with priority, responsibility, and target resolution date. Automated alerts flag items past due.

04

Reports generated & signed off

Branded PDF inspection reports generated directly from the live workspace. Full audit trail maintained throughout.

05

Phase tracker closes the deal

GO / GO WITH CONDITIONS / NO-GO status confirmed across all six transition phases. Every stakeholder sees the same recommendation at the same time.
