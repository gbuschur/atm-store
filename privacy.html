1. Who We Are

ATM Platform is operated by Liberated Blade LLC, a Texas limited liability company at 5900 Balcones Drive #25160, Austin, TX 78731. For privacy questions contact geoffrey@liberatedblade.com.

2. What We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Contact information — name, company, job title, email, phone provided at purchase or intake
  • Payment information — billing name and address. Card details are processed directly by Stripe and never stored by ATM Platform
  • Aircraft and transaction data — aircraft identifiers, maintenance records, inspection findings, open items, and other data you enter into your workspace
  • Communications — emails or messages you send to ATM Platform support

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data — pages visited on store.aerotalentpro.com, browser type, referring URL, and general location
  • Cookies — see Section 6 for details

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver the Service — provision workspace access, send order confirmations, manage your subscription
  • Communicate with you — send service emails, workspace delivery notifications, and support responses
  • Send product updates — notify active subscribers of platform changes (opt out any time)
  • Process payments — pass billing information to Stripe
  • Improve the platform — analyze aggregated, de-identified usage patterns
  • Benchmarking — use aggregated, de-identified transaction data (aircraft type categories, phase durations, finding frequencies) for product development. No individually identifiable data is used for this purpose
  • Legal compliance — retain records as required by law

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited circumstances:

6. Cookies

  • Essential cookies — required for checkout and store functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — used in aggregate to understand site traffic. Do not affect your ability to purchase.
  • Marketing cookies — we do not use advertising or tracking cookies for marketing.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Purchase and contact records — retained for 7 years for accounting and legal compliance

You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting geoffrey@liberatedblade.com. We may retain certain information where required by law.

8. Your Rights

Contact geoffrey@liberatedblade.com to exercise any right. We will respond within 30 days.
